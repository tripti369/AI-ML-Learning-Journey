## Workflow Explanation

### 1. Unlabeled Data

Raw data is collected without predefined categories or labels.

### 2. Pattern Discovery

Algorithms search for hidden structures and relationships within the data.

### 3. Clustering / Dimensionality Reduction

Techniques such as K-Means, Hierarchical Clustering, and PCA are applied.

### 4. Insights Generation

Useful patterns and group characteristics are identified.

### 5. Decision Making

Organizations use the generated insights to improve strategies, products, and services.
