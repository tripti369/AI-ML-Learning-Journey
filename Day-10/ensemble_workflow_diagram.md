# Ensemble Learning Workflow

```

                +----------------+
                |    Dataset     |
                +----------------+
                         |
                         v
        +--------------------------------+
        | Train Multiple ML Models       |
        +--------------------------------+
                         |
                         v
      +--------------------------------------+
      | Each Model Makes Its Own Prediction  |
      +--------------------------------------+
                         |
                         v
      +--------------------------------------+
      | Combine All Predictions              |
      | (Voting / Averaging / Weighted Vote) |
      +--------------------------------------+
                         |
                         v
        +--------------------------------+
        | Final Prediction Generated     |
        +--------------------------------+
                         |
                         v
      +--------------------------------------+
      | Improved Accuracy & Better Stability |
      +--------------------------------------+

```
