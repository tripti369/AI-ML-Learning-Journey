# Case Study

## Credit Card Fraud Detection using XGBoost

### Problem

Banks process millions of transactions every day.

Among these transactions, only a small percentage are fraudulent.

Detecting fraud quickly is important to reduce financial losses.

---

## Why Choose XGBoost?

XGBoost performs well on structured tabular data.

It can identify complex relationships between transaction features and fraudulent behavior.

It also handles imbalanced datasets better than many traditional algorithms.

---

## Why is XGBoost Better Than a Single Decision Tree?

A single Decision Tree may:

- Overfit the training data
- Miss complex patterns
- Produce unstable predictions

XGBoost combines many trees together.

Each tree corrects the mistakes of the previous one, resulting in a stronger model.

---

## Advantages

- High prediction accuracy
- Faster training
- Handles missing values
- Built-in regularization
- Reduces overfitting
- Excellent performance on large datasets

---

## Drawbacks

- More complex than simple models
- Hyperparameter tuning is required
- Higher computational cost

---

## Why Companies Prefer XGBoost

Many banks and fintech companies use XGBoost because it provides:

- Accurate fraud detection
- Low false positives
- Fast prediction
- Reliable performance on real-world data

---

## Conclusion

For fraud detection, XGBoost is one of the best choices because it combines high accuracy, speed, and robustness while reducing overfitting compared to a single Decision Tree.
