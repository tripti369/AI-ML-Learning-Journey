# CNN Case Study

## Application: Cat vs Dog Classification

### Input

Thousands of labeled images of cats and dogs.

---

### Features Learned by CNN

Early Layers:
- Edges
- Corners
- Simple textures

Middle Layers:
- Eyes
- Nose
- Fur patterns
- Ears

Deep Layers:
- Entire face
- Body shape
- Tail
- Overall animal appearance

---

### Why CNN is Better than Traditional Neural Network?

- Preserves image structure.
- Learns important visual features automatically.
- Requires fewer parameters.
- More accurate for image classification.
- Better at handling large image datasets.

---

### Challenges

- Requires large datasets.
- Needs powerful GPUs.
- Can overfit with limited data.
- Computationally expensive.
- Sensitive to poor image quality.

---

## Conclusion

CNNs can accurately distinguish between cats and dogs by automatically learning meaningful image features, making them ideal for image classification tasks.
